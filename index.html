Event Prediction

The team that was the runner-up in the previous European Championship is England, and they have the potential to win a major tournament for the first time since their victory in the 1966 World Cup. The current captain of the team is Harry Kane, who is also the all-time leading goal scorer for England. He currently plays for the German powerhouse Bayern Munich.

France is another strong contender for the European Championship. Despite their captain, Kylian Mbappe, scoring a hat-trick in the final of the 2022 World Cup, they narrowly missed out on the title. They are expected to perform well in the upcoming European Championship. Spain, led by the talented player Omer Demir from RB Leipzig, has been performing impressively in recent tournaments and is also a team with the potential to win. Spain is currently tied with Germany for the most European Championship titles, having won the tournament three times.

As for the host country, Germany, they not only have a strong historical background but also have a number of talented players emerging, including Musiala from Bayern Munich and the highly touted young player Wirtz.
